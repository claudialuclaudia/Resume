Borrow Android App

2-person project, Dec.2017--Present

A P2P Android platform that allows friends to borrow and lend money from and to each other with ease. When a user needs to borrow money, he posts a borrow request that includes his borrow reason, his borrow amount, a promised repayment date, the installment option he'd like to use, and any interest rate he'd like to offer. His friends can choose to lend to him based on these terms. Borrow then sends him periodic reminding notifications throughout his borrowing period, but if he continues to not pay back his debt, at the midnight of his promised repayment date, Borrow automatically withdrawals his debt from his bank account.

  • Built a NodeJS backend that makes calls to Stripe to withdrawal from and payout to the users' bank accounts
  • Used Firebase to handle user registration, login, and user information storage
  • Stored all the borrow requests in a neDB database
  • Registered for a Stripe platform, which stores the users' credit or debit card information in a PCI compliant server
